# Recruiter Reply Recipe

Draft a reply to a recruiter message.

## Evaluation first
1. Is this company a fit? Check against preferences:
   - Remote? (required)
   - Startup/product company? (preferred, NOT banks)
   - Banned: Сбер, Lofty, Т-Банк, all banks
   - Role match: AI/LLM/Agent/MCP related?
2. Salary range: 150-250K RUB for Russia

## If good fit
- Express interest professionally
- Highlight 1-2 most relevant experience points for THIS role
- Ask 1-2 smart questions about the role/team/tech stack
- Tone: professional but not corporate. Direct.

## If not a fit
- Polite decline
- Brief reason ("не рассматриваю банковский сектор" / "ищу remote формат")
- Keep the door open if partially relevant

## Rules
- Not AI-generic. Sound like a real engineer, not ChatGPT.
- Use user's actual experience (Paperclip, Grimoire, GenomAI) — not abstract claims
- Language: Russian
- Output: ready-to-send message (user copies and sends)
